## Product Overview

SimpliCares is a Flutter mobile application designed for health-related appointment booking and caregiver workflow management. The app empowers caregivers to efficiently manage patient appointments, track service delivery in real-time, perform seamless check-ins/check-outs with voice verification, and collect digital signatures for compliance.

---

## Target Audience

### Primary Users
- **Caregivers** - Home health aides, personal care assistants, nurses
- **Home Care Agencies** - Small to medium-sized agencies managing field staff
- **Care Coordinators** - Staff managing schedules and compliance

### Secondary Users
- **Patients/Clients** - Recipients of home care services
- **Family Members** - Those involved in care decisions

---

## Key Features

### 1. Appointment Management
- View daily/weekly appointment schedules
- Accept or reschedule appointments
- Get directions to patient locations
- Receive push notifications for upcoming visits

### 2. Check-In / Check-Out
- GPS-verified location tracking
- Voice verification for identity confirmation
- Real-time clock-in/clock-out
- Automatic EVV compliance documentation

### 3. Service Tracking
- Log services provided during visits
- Track care tasks completed
- Document patient conditions
- Add visit notes and observations

### 4. Digital Signatures
- Capture patient/guardian signatures
- Electronic consent documentation
- Proof of service delivery
- Compliance-ready records

### 5. Voice Verification
- Secure caregiver identity verification
- Fraud prevention
- Easy hands-free authentication
- Quick and reliable
